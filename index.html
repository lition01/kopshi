Welcome to Bota Feminore

Where little minds bloom and grow in a nurturing, safe environment filled with love, learning, and laughter.

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Bota Feminore has been a cornerstone of early childhood education in our community. Our name, meaning "Children's World" in Albanian, reflects our commitment to creating a magical environment where every child can thrive.

We believe that the early years are the most important in a child's development. Our experienced educators provide personalized attention to help each child develop confidence, creativity, and a lifelong love of learning.
